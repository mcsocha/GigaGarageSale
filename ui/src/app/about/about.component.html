Our Story

GigaGarageSale was founded by Michael Socha and Danny Xiong, two super dedicated computer help desk technician coworkers at Harvard. While assisting faculty, staff, and students with their computer issues, Michael noticed a recurring trend: many students were in need of affordable computer parts while others were upgrading their systems, leaving their old but still functional components unused.

This observation spurred on Danny, being an already accomplished E-Bay reseller, to work with Michael to create a dedicated marketplace where gently used computer parts could be bought and sold, providing a solution for budget-conscious students and tech enthusiasts alike.

Our Mission

At GigaGarageSale, our mission is to promote sustainability and affordability in the tech community. We believe that every computer part has a story and a potential second life. Our platform allows users to:

  • Sell their gently used computer parts, helping others save money.
  • Find quality components at a fraction of the retail price.
  • Reduce electronic waste by giving old parts a new home.

Join Our Community

We are more than just a marketplace; we are a community of computer enthusiasts and eco-conscious individuals. By joining GigaGarageSale, you become part of a network that values quality, affordability, and sustainability.
